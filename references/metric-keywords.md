# Metric Keywords

Use these keyword families when extracting data from annual/interim reports.

## Revenue / Segments
- total revenue
- segment revenue
- business mix
- transport services
- station commercial
- property rental and management
- property development profit
- mainland china and international

## Leverage / Liquidity
- gross debt
- net debt
- net debt-to-equity
- gearing ratio
- cash and deposit balance
- undrawn committed facilities
- liquidity

## Financing
- weighted average cost of borrowings
- borrowing cost
- interest cover
- medium term notes
- perpetual capital securities
- green bond
- sustainable finance
